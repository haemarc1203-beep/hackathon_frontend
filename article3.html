Trading platform

There are two main types of trading platforms:

  • Commercial platforms for general users
  • Proprietary platforms developed by financial institutions for their own use.

Before you can trade stocks online you have to select an online broker. Your online broker will execute your trades and store your money and stock in an account.

You can open 2 kinds of accounts with a brokerage:

  • Cash account: buy with your straightforward checking account. You need to already have enough money in your account to pay for a stock.
  • Margin account: more like a loan or credit. You can borrow money from the brokerage, using the equity of the stock you already own as collateral. Buying on credit introduces more financial risks.

Once you have made all these choices, you must fund your account. You can make a deposit by check, make a wire transfer to the brokerage or transfer holdings from another brokerage.
