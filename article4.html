Buying/selling stocks, trading, and making investments!

Stock data: You can find information about stocks in 2 ways

  1. Real time stock quote
  2. Delayed stock quote

There are different ways in which you can arrange to buy a stock with your broker:

  • Market order: executes at the current market price of the stock.
  • Limit order: executes at or better than a price you specify. If the price doesn’t reach the set price, the trade will not go through.

Some brokerages offer additional options:

  • Stop order
  • Stop limit order
  • Trailing stop order
