Brokers, Clients, Stocks

What is a broker?

A broker is a licensed person or firm that buys and sells securities on behalf of clients. They can provide access to markets and tools for trading.

What is an online Trading Platform?

An online trading platform lets you place buy/sell orders through an app or website. Some platforms offer charts, research, and educational resources.

What can you buy from a broker besides stocks?

Depending on the platform, you can trade ETFs, mutual funds, bonds, options, and more. Always understand fees and risk before investing.
